About Me

My name is Zahnen Garner, and I'm a web development student based in Portland, Oregon. I am a self-starting, enthusiastic individual with strong communication, teamworking, problem solving, and multitasking skills. I have a sharp eye for detail and a fervent desire to learn.

I have spent the better part of the last decade in retail or hospitality, with my most recent role focusing specifically on hotel sales & digital marketing. In my experience as a Sales & Marketing Coordinator, I was afforded opportunities to recreate outdated digital materials such as reservation confirmations emails, marketing email templates, and other customer-facing collateral. It was here that I discovered my keen interest in these (relatively small) coding projects.

I've been curious to learn more about coding and web development, and decided to finally make the jump. In committing to a full-time program, I hope to learn all that I possibly can over course of the class and share that progress with the world here.
